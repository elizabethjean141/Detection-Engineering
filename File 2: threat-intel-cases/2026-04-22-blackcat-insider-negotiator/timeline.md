# BlackCat Insider Negotiator — Timeline

## Pre-Incident Context

- **November 2021** — BlackCat/ALPHV ransomware operation emerges as RaaS
- **2021–2023** — BlackCat linked by FBI to 60+ breaches; over 1,000 victims; $300M+ in ransom payments through September 2023

## Conspiracy Activity

- **April 2023** — Martino begins abusing his role at DigitalMint to assist BlackCat operators
- **April 2023 – November 2023** — Martino, Martin (DigitalMint), and Goldberg (Sygnia) operate as BlackCat affiliates, paying 20% cut to BlackCat operators, launching attacks against at least 5 US organizations
- **2023** — Victim payments include:
  - $26.79M from a nonprofit
  - $25.66M from a financial services firm
  - Additional victims: law firms, school districts, medical facilities, other financial services
  - $1.2M from one victim was split among the three and laundered

## Law Enforcement Disruption

- **Late 2023** — Law enforcement disrupts BlackCat/ALPHV globally by seizing the gang's data leak site

## Investigation & Prosecution

- **April 2025** — FBI informs DigitalMint of their investigation into Martino; DigitalMint terminates him the following day
- **April 2025** — Martino ceases affiliate activity (per court documents, activity window ran through April 2025)
- **October 2025** — Initial indictment names Martin and Goldberg; Martino is referenced as "Co-Conspirator 1" (unnamed)
- **December 2025** — Martin and Goldberg plead guilty
- **March 2026** — Court documents unsealed naming Martino
- **April 21, 2026** — Martino pleads guilty to conspiracy to obstruct commerce by extortion
- **April 30, 2026** — Martin and Goldberg sentencing (scheduled)
- **July 9, 2026** — Martino sentencing (scheduled)

## Asset Recovery

- **~$10M seized from Martino**, including cryptocurrency, vehicles, a food truck, and a luxury fishing boat

## Dwell Time Analysis

From the perspective of the victims:

- **Active leaks:** April 2023 — for each victim, dwell time within their negotiation = the full duration of that negotiation (days to weeks)
- **From first conspiracy act to first law enforcement action:** April 2023 → April 2025 = **~24 months**
- **From conspiracy end to indictment:** April 2025 → October 2025 = **~6 months**

The defenders were never going to catch this from inside. The investigation was entirely external. Total time from first crime to public accountability: **~3 years**.
